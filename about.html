About Me

Hi, I'm Maryam Abdulkarim, a passionate UI/UX designer with 5+ years of experience. I love creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that solve real-world problems.

My design philosophy is centered around simplicity, clarity, and consistency. I believe that good design should be accessible and easy to use for everyone, regardless of their technical skills.

Throughout my career, I have worked with a wide range of clients, from startups to established companies, and have delivered successful projects in various industries, including healthcare, education, and e-commerce.
